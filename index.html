Responsible Gambling

Your wellbeing is our priority.

Gambling Should Be Fun — Know the Risks

Online gambling is a form of entertainment. However, for some people it can become a serious problem. Betivo.casino is committed to promoting responsible gambling and providing resources to those who need help.

Warning Signs of Problem Gambling

  • Spending more money than you can afford to lose;
  • Gambling to escape stress, anxiety, or other problems;
  • Lying to family or friends about gambling habits;
  • Chasing losses by continuing to gamble;
  • Neglecting work, family, or other responsibilities because of gambling;
  • Feeling anxious, irritable, or depressed when not gambling.

Tools Available at Licensed Casinos

All casinos we recommend offer responsible gambling tools. Use them proactively:

  • Deposit limits: Set daily, weekly, or monthly limits on your deposits.
  • Loss limits: Cap the amount you can lose in a given period.
  • Session time limits: Set reminders or hard stops on play sessions.
  • Self-exclusion: Temporarily or permanently exclude yourself from the casino.
  • Reality checks: Receive periodic reminders of how long you have been playing.
